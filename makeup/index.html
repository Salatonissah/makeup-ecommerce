Our Tea Journey

Founded in 2010, Leaf & Brew began as a passion project between two tea enthusiasts who wanted to share the world's finest teas while supporting sustainable farming practices.

Today, we partner directly with over 50 small family farms across Asia, Africa, and South America to bring you teas of exceptional quality and character.
